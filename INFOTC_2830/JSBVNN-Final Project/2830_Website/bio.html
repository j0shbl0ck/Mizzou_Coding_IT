Capturing Time

What is impossible, possible.

My passion for photography started around the age of 14 when wanting to capture time.
People say it's impossible to capture time, you can't hold it, or hear it.
But what if I could show you it?
Through the lens, I can produce the seconds you just lived in a still image.
In a few seconds, you will be able to recall the sounds of laughter and enjoyment.
